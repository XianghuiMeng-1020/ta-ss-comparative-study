\[
250.0 \, \text{meters} \div 4 = 62.5 \, \text{meters}
\]

So, each of the four pieces is **62.5 meters long**.

\[
4 \div 2 = 2 \, \text{pieces}
\]

\[
62.5 \, \text{meters} \div 2 = 31.25 \, \text{meters}

\[
