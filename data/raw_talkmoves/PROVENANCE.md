# TalkMoves Dataset

- Source: https://github.com/SumnerLab/TalkMoves
- Paper: Suresh et al. (2022). The TalkMoves Dataset: K-12 Mathematics Lesson Transcripts Annotated for Teacher and Student Discursive Moves. LREC 2022.
- License: CC BY-NC-SA 4.0
- Files:
    talkmoves_train_data.csv   – 504 training sentences with talk-move labels
    talkmoves_transcripts_all.csv – all raw transcript xlsx files compiled
    talkmoves_student_labels.csv  – sentence-pair + student move labels
    TalkMoves_repo/              – full repository clone
- Description: 567 human-annotated K-12 mathematics lesson transcripts with sentence-level labels for 10 discursive moves (accountable talk theory) and dialog-act labels.
- Relevance: Classroom-level ecological context and cross-validated talk-move taxonomy bridging tutor-move categories in MathDial to broader classroom discourse research.
