# Eedi Question-Anchored Tutoring Dialogues (QATD-2k)

- Source: https://huggingface.co/datasets/Eedi/Question-Anchored-Tutoring-Dialogues-2k
- Paper: Zent, Smith, Woodhead (2025). PIIvot: A Lightweight NLP Anonymization Framework. EMNLP 2025.
- License: CC BY-NC-SA 4.0
- Configs downloaded: anchored-dialogues, dq-question-metadata
- Description: 2,000 real 1:1 chat-based math tutoring sessions from the Eedi platform (UK, students age ~12, Nov 2021 – Feb 2025), with talk-move predictions and PII anonymization.
- Relevance: Large-scale real platform data enabling cross-platform robustness analysis against MathDial and Bridge.
